About Us

The institution is running under the supervision of “Kesar Shikshan Sansthan Kesar Shikshan Sansthan (K.S.S.)” is a voluntary organisation which acts in the field of primary education. Since 1980 K.S.S. has been working in this field and has done laudable works for the primary section specially in rural areas of the district. The aim of the academy is to provide a well disciplined educational environment to students where they can rise completely. It is a complete residential school for boys and girls of class Nursery to std (IX). There is no seat for day scholars. Our Vision

We aim to develop children whose faces reflect hope, whose bodies possess strength, whose minds have strong willpower, whose lives embody self-reliance, and whose hearts are eager to gain knowledge. They should have respect for life, devotion toward family, loyalty toward the nation, and reverence for their ancestors.

Our Aspiration

A child, in seed form, is the manifestation of the Divine. With a pure and suitable environment, we aspire to nurture children with numerical and literacy skills along with good behavior, proper communication, morality, discipline in life, and values rooted in devotion. With this aspiration, we have moved forward on this noble path.

Our Educational Objective

The sole objective of the institution is to provide quality education through proficient and trained teachers in both Hindi and English medium, with the aim of character building. Special attention is given to the complete development of children’s mental, physical, social, and spiritual abilities so that they become capable of contributing to their own welfare, the welfare of society, and humanity, and are able to lead healthy and meaningful lives.

Our Commitment

Children possess infinite potential for development. They are carriers of our cultural traditions and also the future leaders of the nation. The best form of education is that which brings out the inherent abilities of children so they can distinguish between truth and falsehood, right and wrong, through their own capabilities. Although development has no limits, the school is committed to equipping itself with the most modern educational systems for the purpose of personality development of students.

A message from our Director

A child’s development is a dynamic process of overall growth that continues throughout life. In a child’s development, the role of parents is as important as that of the school because, under this broad concept of education, parents are also teachers of the child. Their influence holds a highly significant place in the child’s development because the child spends most of their time with them.
